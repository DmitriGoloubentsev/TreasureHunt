# The Final Challenge

You're almost there, Red Dragons!

To unlock the **Grand Finale**, you need help from ALL the other teams!

## Your Mission:

1. Find **Blue Wizards** (Team 2) - get their letter
2. Find **Green Explorers** (Team 3) - get their letter
3. Find **Yellow Stars** (Team 4) - get their letter
4. Find **Purple Ninjas** (Team 5) - get their letter

## Combine the Letters

Put all 4 letters together in team order (2, 3, 4, 5) to form the final code!

**Hint:** It's something you eat at a birthday party!
